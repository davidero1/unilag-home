About UNILAG

Founded in 1962, the University of Lagos
has, for over 5 decades, provided qualitative
and research-oriented education to Nigerians
and all those who have entered its domain in
search of knowledge. The University has
built a legacy of excellence and has been
instrumental in the production of top range
graduates and academia who have had
tremendous impact, directly or indirectly, on
growth and development in Nigeria.
